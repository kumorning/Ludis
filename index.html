LUDIS

UX/UI, Mobile Application

LUDIS is a Virtual Pacemaker that offers users to stay motivated and achieve their personal fitness goals – virtually. Since last year, everyone has suffered one way or another from COVID-19. And for many, a lockdown was put into effect immediately across the world. While it's essential to protect our health and promote well-being at this vulnerable time, physical contact is inhibited, and finding the daily motivation for the right exercise is strenuous. LUDIS provides various workout routines for users and keeps users motivated throughout their workout achievements.

Key Feature 01 Running
Recommended routes for your daliy runs

Key Feature 02 | Full body workout
Daily curated full body workouts to stay fit

Ludis also provides workout missions everyday. There workout will help you stay fit and motivated as you achieve daily goals and streaks.

Key Feature 03 | Challenge
Virtual running with friends

Challenge your friends to exercise with you LIVE. It's like a game – invite friends, set challenge and start. Winner of each round will get the privilege to start their music playlist.
